Say what?

The internship program gives you the opportunity to spend the summer working directly at GoodData with our development teams and business operations departments. You‘ll gain hands-on experience and also have the opportunity to become our full-time employee.

Job description

You will be given a real project to work on, as well as receive mentoring from the team. We're a small company, so you'll see everything that goes into software; not just code, but testing, documentation, customer support, and user interface design – stuff you would never learn at a bigger company!

TL;DR

You are eligible if you are a university / college student looking for a summer job in the software industry. The internship lasts between 8 and 12 weeks, starting from July and the position is located in Prague.

What we expect from you

  • Integration of vulnerability scanners into our testing framework
  • Improvement of Ruby library used by our customers
  • Tooling enhancements
  • Other great things you‘ll fancy doing

What we require

  • fluency of English (both written and spoken)
  • understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • 1337 programming skills with at least one of the following: Ruby, Python, Java
  • passion for coding and debugging
